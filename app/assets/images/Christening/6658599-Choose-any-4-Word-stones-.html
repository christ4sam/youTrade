Description

Set of 4 word stones in a presentation box.

Please choose 4 word stones and email me with your choice.
These can be for that special friend in your life to show that you care - a little reminder, or a little help. They can make a wonderful gift for a Baprtism, Christening, First Communion, Confirmation or as Wedding or Christening favours.

They are made from a buff stoneware clay, textured and then inscribed before being bisque fired. I then paint them with a black matt glaze in order to highlight the inscription and use a shiny glaze on the top right corner. They are glaze fired to 1220C.

To keep in your pocket or purse or put somewhere special - for someone special - too show that you care.

Approximately: 5.0 to 6.0cm (2 to 2 1/2 inches) long, 2.5cm (1 inch) high.

Gift Box included.

(I can make these in quantity and personalized. A discount can be offered and the postage will be calculated accordingly.
Working with clay requires a long lead time due to the drying process so please bear this in mind. I would be more than happy to answer any questions. Please get in touch via the shop.)

Inspiration

I began making Word Stones when my youngest daughter started secondary (high) school. I wanted to give her a little extra strength and encouragement, so I made her a 'hug' to keep in her pocket. I got the idea from J.M. Barrie's novel Peter Pan, when Wendy gave Peter a 'kiss' - a thimble.

* This is a memento - NOT A TOY. Please remember that babies and young children can choke on small objects.

* The one you see in the photo is the one that you will receive.
